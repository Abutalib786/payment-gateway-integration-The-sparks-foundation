About Us

Spark foundation is an NGO for poor child education in India. It helps over 10,00,000 childrens and their their families every year.It has projects on education, healthcare, livelihood and women empowerment, in remote villages and slums across differnet states of india. From the words of Jawaharlal Nehru,children are like bunds in the garden that need to be carefully nurterd, as they are future citizens of tommorow. Education acts as the wind beneath the wings for children for grow as healthy and prosperous adults in the future.
